Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Bay Information Systems Limited is registered in England and Wales. This policy describes what data we collect when you use this website and how we use it.

What We Collect

If you submit the contact form on this site, we collect your name, organisation, and message. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry. It is not shared with any third party, used for marketing, or retained beyond the duration of the relevant correspondence.

This site records anonymous behavioural events -- pages visited, buttons clicked -- for the purpose of understanding how the site is used. These events contain no personally identifiable information, set no cookies, and are not shared with third parties. The system used for this is Clientlog, operated by Bay Information Systems.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. No cookie consent banner is required.

Third Parties

This site loads assets from the following third-party CDNs: Bootstrap (jsdelivr.net) and Font Awesome (cdnjs.cloudflare.com). These services may log your IP address as part of standard CDN operation. We do not control or have access to those logs.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Given the limited data we collect, such requests can be resolved by contacting us directly at ed@bayis.co.uk.
